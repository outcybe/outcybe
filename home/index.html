Outbursts are the rawest material you'll find here. Written ravings that might ripen into an Outgrowth or shoot straight up to Output Plaza

Welcome to Output Plaza, my rinky dink little Sankofa Square at the end of the world. Outgrowths that have any hope of return on investment end up here, and as much as I hate to admit it, I have a metabolism and overhead costs. The good news is, when you buy my stuff, I make more stuff. You can't tell me you don't like stuff.

Once I've sufficiently hyperventillated and come down from an Outburst, it might end up here. And if you write an email to outcybe@proton.me saying "can you give this corporeal form so I can buy it for Christ's sake?"
