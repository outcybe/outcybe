War Is Brewing

Need to make some graphics that tap into the current moment the TPS are having. What we know is that a public relations war is brewing after negotiations between the Toronto Police Association and the police board broke down and went to an arbitration process expected to begin in November. The same weapons of mass propaganda that were used to pressure the city for that additional $12 million in the 2024 budget will come out again, possibly in even greater force, depending on how much money is on the line and to what extent public pressure will impact the arbitration process.
