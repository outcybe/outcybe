Whimsickle: Socialism In Its Fantasy Era

I knew the 21st century had gone too far when my dad told me about an active meme warzone I'd never heard of. At this point in the internet's festering history, I was a host all but entirely poisoned and rejected by my Facebook feed only a decade after the birth of social media, whereas my dad's online life was a newly flourishing Enlightenment era salon of intellectual debate, jest and Wordle.

The two factions of memes he told me about were warring over whether we're living in George Orwell's "1984" totalitarian thoughtcrime police state, or the hedonistic gilded cage of Aldous Huxley's "Brave New World."
